Low Carb Ground Beef

Ingredients

 - Green Peppers
 - Sweet onions
 - 1 lb ground beef
 - 1 tbsp olive oil
 - 1 oz cheddar cheese
 - 1 cup romaine lettuce
 - 1 grape tomatoe

Chop peppers and onions into small chunks and place in frying pan over medium heat with 1 tbsp of oil. Once cooked remove peppers and onions. Add groud beef cook until brown. Add back peppers and onions and continue to cook until done.

Serve over a bed of romaine lettuce topped with cheddar cheese and 1 grape tomatoe.
